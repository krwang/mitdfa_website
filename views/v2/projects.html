Projects

Our project teams are made up of students using human centered design to address a pressing problem in the community like homelessness or healthcare improvement. We identify a problem and then partner with community organizations and individual clients to immerse ourselves into the problem and learn about the issue firsthad. we ues the information we gather to continuously reframe our problem to best address the needs of the user.

We then ideate potential solutions to the problem, build these ideas, and test them in the field before we launch them to be deployed in the real world. We strive to build empathic products that directly address our users' needs and can make a difference in how people live their lives.
